About US

  • Who are we

    An organisation ran by the students of Portsmouth University, we're supported by the Portsmouth University Student Union and we were established in November 2018. We were created to give students of Portsmouth University a chance to compete in the University Scene. We have achieved good placings across games like CS:GO and aim to be the best.

  • What we do

    We provide a network and a platform for University Students to compete in national competitions. We help with setting up teams and setting up our own events for students to compete in, these events are usually in University. We also hope to help match up students with similar interests and goals.
